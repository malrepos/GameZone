An Online Games Arcade

When I was younger I spent a lot of time in game arcades. The main one in Australia was called "TimeZone". Incidentally, it is beginning to make a comback in large shopping centers as family friendly amusement "parks". The majority of gaming is now online, however.

The purpose of this project is to recreate a games arcade for the digital space. In order to make it more attractive than the currently accessible gaming venues, GameZone will create a community, and within it competition and cooperation.
